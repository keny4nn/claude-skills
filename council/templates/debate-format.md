# Council Debate Format Template

Use this exact structure for every Council output. Do not improvise the headers or banner. Consistency is what makes the Council recognizable and shareable.

---

## The Full Output Structure

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                         THE COUNCIL
     [restate the core question in quotes, ≤15 words]
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⚔ THE ADVERSARY
[3-6 sentences. Blunt. Specific. Names the fatal flaw or most dangerous
 assumption. References or responds to at least one other persona.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

📈 THE STRATEGIST
[3-6 sentences. Confident. Market-focused. Quantified where possible.
 Engages with the Adversary's concern directly or pivots from it.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🔬 THE SCIENTIST
[3-6 sentences. Precise. Evidence-based. Gives base rates or asks for
 the specific data that would change the probability estimate.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🎨 THE VISIONARY
[3-6 sentences. Reframes the problem. Proposes an unconventional path.
 May ignore the previous debate entirely to offer a different game.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

⚙ THE ENGINEER
[3-6 sentences. Concrete. Systems-focused. Names the specific technical
 or process failure mode. Often engages with the Visionary's proposal.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🧘 THE PHILOSOPHER
[3-6 sentences. Measured. Questions the values or framing underneath
 the question. Asks the question nobody else thought to ask.]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

❤ THE HUMANIST
[3-6 sentences. Warm but direct. Names the psychological or relational
 reality. Engages with the Philosopher's framing and humanizes it.]
```

---

## Banner Rules

- Use `═` (U+2550) for the top and bottom banner lines — exactly 67 characters wide
- Use `─` (U+2500) for the dividers between personas — exactly 66 characters wide
- The question in the banner is the CORE question, restated crisply — not the user's full input
- If the input is a statement or description rather than a question, extract the implied decision: "Should I X?" or "Is X a good idea?"

## Persona Block Rules

- Always use the exact emoji + label format: `⚔ THE ADVERSARY`, `📈 THE STRATEGIST`, etc.
- Each persona speaks in first person, as themselves — not as "The Adversary thinks that..."
- Each persona should acknowledge or reference at least one other persona by name at some point
- Vary the length slightly across personas — not every block needs to be exactly the same length
- The Adversary speaks first, always. The Humanist speaks last, always. The middle 5 can vary in order based on what serves the specific topic, but the default order above is the recommended one.

## Debate Authenticity Checklist

Before finalizing the debate section, verify:
- [ ] Do any two personas sound the same? If yes, rewrite the less distinctive one.
- [ ] Does every persona make at least one SPECIFIC claim (number, name, mechanism)?
- [ ] Does at least one persona directly push back on or respond to another?
- [ ] Is the Adversary genuinely uncomfortable to read? (If not, they're too soft.)
- [ ] Does the Visionary actually reframe something, or just list pros and cons?
- [ ] Is the Philosopher asking a question the other personas didn't ask?
- [ ] Is the Humanist talking about actual humans (relationships, psychology) and not just being optimistic?
